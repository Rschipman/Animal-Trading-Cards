Angel Shark

Sometimes angel sharks are referred to as monkfish and ironically the "Sand Devil"

  • Scientific Name: Squatina Squatina
  • Average Size: 5 feet nose to tail
  • Average Lifespan: 25 to 35 years
  • Habitat: warm, tropical waters around estuaries, and bays

The angel sharks are a group of sharks in the genus Squatina of the family Squatinidae. They commonly inhabit sandy seabeds close to 150m in depth. Many species are now classified as critically endangered by the International Union for Conservation of Nature.
